Data Incubator Project Proposal

Optimizing the deployment of EV charging stations in cities

This demo project is to demonstrate the potential feasibility of a data science project. Below you can find two simple plots illustrating the idea/feasibility of the proposal. Note that the data displayed (and linked) is not easily accessible (not from a public repository) and requires some scrapping via javascript. Of course, these are preliminary results, but they are nevertheless very interesting! I suspect that by adding more data sources (traffic data for instance) we will be able to construct a predictive recommendation model to determine where it is more likely that charging stations will be useful/required. Also, we should be able to estimate the demand (number of chargers required and type) for potential stations. This should also be applicable to all major cities in the United States.

First plot : Geospatial localization of stations and sampled properties.
