Optimizing the deployment of EV charging stations in cities

LATEST UPDATE (August 8th)

Alexandre Day, July 23rd 2018

Figure 1 (interactive - hover, drag and scroll !):
Map of the location of the current charging stations (red markers) in the Washington DC metro area along with sampled locations of rental apartements and houses (blue markers). Larger blue markers indicate a greater cost of living (i.e. the cost per square-feet is higher). Larger red markers indicate accessibility to a greater number of charging stations. Hover over the markers to visualize more information such as the price of rental, the number and type of chargers, etc . Note that chargers tend to be distributed on the main road arteries and in the vicinity of the city center.
