Data Incubator project

Optimizing the demand for charging stations in populated areas

Alexandre Day, July 23rd 2018

Figure 1 (interactive - hover and scroll !):
Map of the location of the current charging stations (red markers) in the Washington DC metro area along with sampled locations of rental apartements and houses (blue markers). Larger blue markers indicate a greater cost of living (i.e. the cost per square-feet is higher). Larger red markers indicate accessibility to a greater number of charging stations. Hover over the markers to visualize more information such as the price of rental, the number and type of chargers, etc.
