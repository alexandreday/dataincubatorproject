Figure 1:
Map of the location of the current charging stations (red markers) in the Washington DC metro area and sampled locations of rental apartements and houses (blue markers). Larger blue markers indicate an larger cost of living (i.e. the cost per square-foot is higher). Larger red markers indicate accessibility to a greater number of chargers. Hover over the markers to visualize more information such as the price of rental, the number and type of chargers, etc.
